About this project

This interactive timeline traces how artificial intelligence tools have been deployed across the Japanese animation industry — the production experiments, the tools and startups, the fan and labor controversies, the legal disputes, and the government policy that have shaped the conversation from 2016 to 2026. Each entry links directly to primary reporting so readers can follow the sources and reach their own conclusions.
